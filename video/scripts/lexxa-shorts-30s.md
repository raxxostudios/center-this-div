# Lexxa Script - YouTube Shorts (30s)

## Production Notes
- Voice: Lexxa starts CHILL, switches to HYPE at the result
- Overlay on Remotion Shorts render (out/shorts.mp4)
- More breathing room than TikTok. Let the visuals land.
- Text overlays: key stats punch in as Lexxa says them
- Music: low ambient drone, builds tension during drag, drops on result

---

## Script

**[0:00-0:03]** [CHILL]
"Can you center a div? Like, actually center it?"

> VISUAL: Clean black screen. Text types in: "Can you center a div?" Then a beat. Question mark blinks.

**[0:03-0:06]** [CHILL]
"The success threshold is zero point zero zero zero one pixels. Five thousand times smaller than a Retina pixel."

> VISUAL: "0.0001px" scales up massive. Then shrinks to a dot. Size comparison graphic.

**[0:06-0:09]** [CHILL]
"Nobody has ever won. That counter is real. It pulls from the database."

> VISUAL: Success counter "0" pulses. Database icon subtle in corner.

**[0:09-0:14]** [CHILL]
"Watch. You drag the div. The HUD tracks your deviation to six decimal places. Every pixel matters."

> VISUAL: Gameplay starts. Cursor grabs div, starts dragging. HUD numbers cascade in real-time. Radar sweep visible.

**[0:14-0:19]** [CHILL -> HYPE transition]
"Getting close now. One point two pixels. That sounds close, right?"

> VISUAL: Div near center. Numbers slowing down. Measurement lines visible. Player blips on radar.

**[0:19-0:22]** [HYPE]
"NOT CENTERED. Your one point two pixels is twenty-four thousand kilometers on Earth. You missed the Pacific Ocean."

> VISUAL: Submit. "NOT CENTERED" glitch slam. Earth Scale zooms out showing distance. Quote appears.

**[0:22-0:26]** [HYPE]
"The leaderboard? Closest is zero point zero two pixels. Still two hundred times over the threshold."

> VISUAL: Leaderboard scrolls. Top entries glow. Gap between best and threshold visualized.

**[0:26-0:30]** [HYPE]
"Three thousand attempts. Fourteen countries. Zero successes. Ever. Try it."

> VISUAL: Success counter "0" holds. center-this-div.vercel.app fades in. RAXXO logo.
